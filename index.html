About Us

Beau's Bonbons is a small candy shop located in the heart of Ottawa offering all kinds of sweets to satisfy anyone's sweet tooth! Established in 1974, Beau's Bonbons have been providing Ottawans with locally crafted goodies for 42 years and counting!
